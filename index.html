About Vikas Educational Trust and Vikas Central School

Founded in 2007-2008 by a group of visionary educationists, The Vikas Educational Trust was established with a mission to preserve and nurture our rich educational heritage while evolving to meet the needs of today’s learners. This vision materialized with the launch of Vikas Central School, offering State syllabus education with a focus on academic excellence and holistic development.

Starting with just 60 students, the school has grown significantly over the years and now proudly educates over 1,000 students. In 2024, we celebrated our 17th Anniversary, marking nearly two decades of dedication to shaping young minds.

Vikas Early Learning Academy – Building Strong Foundations At Vikas, our Early Learning Academy is committed to fostering social-emotional development, early literacy, and numeracy skills in young children. Our nurturing environment ensures that every student builds the character and confidence needed to thrive in school and life.

We equip our learners with practical knowledge and real-world skills, preparing them to stand out and succeed in the industries and communities they will one day serve

Vikas Teaching Methodology

At Vikas, we combine international-standard education with a newly renovated, child-friendly campus — all at an affordable cost. Our unique teaching approach encourages hands-on learning, creativity, and critical thinking, ensuring every child grows with confidence and curiosity. At Vikas, every journey is one of discovery and endless possibilities.

Step By Step Systematic Education

Smart Board Learning

Our classrooms are equipped with Smart Board technology to make learning interactive and engaging. Digital tools enhance understanding and help students grasp complex concepts.

Computer Lab

Library
